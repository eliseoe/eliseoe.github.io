<p>I’m Elise Özalp, an applied ML researcher working on machine learning for dynamical systems.</p>

<p>I completed my PhD (Jan 2022 – Nov 2025) at Imperial College London (<a href="https://magrilab.ae.ic.ac.uk/">Magrilab</a>, <a href="https://www.imperial.ac.uk/aeronautics/">Aeronautics</a>; Supervisor: Prof. Luca Magri), where I developed physics-informed ML methods for real-time forecasting of partially observed chaotic systems. My research combined latent-space models, recurrent neural networks, and data assimilation to recover long-term statistics and stability properties.</p>

<p>Most recently, I was an AI Fellow (Faculty.ai & Inigo, Oct 2025 – Dec 2025), where I built a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipeline for loss analysis.</p>

<p>If you’d like to collaborate or chat, email me at <a href="mailto:eliseoezalp@gmail.com">eliseoezalp@gmail.com</a>.</p>
