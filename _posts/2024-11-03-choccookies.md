## Ingredients (for 15? cookies)

- 115g unsalted butter, melted and cooled
- 50g light brown sugar
- 50 g granulated sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 155g all-purpose flour
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 200g dark chocolate chopped
- a pinch of salt

## Preparation

1. Preheat the oven to 175˚C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Combine the melted butter with the brown and granulated sugars. Whisk until it forms a thick, homogenous paste. Add the egg and vanilla, mix well.
4. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet batter until just combined. Add chocolate and mix with a spatula. 
5. Scoop out cookies - I used about 30g each. 
6. Bake for 12 minutes, until golden and crispy on the edges and gooey in the center.
